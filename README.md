# 📊 Northwind Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built using the Northwind dataset to analyze business performance across sales, customers, and products.

The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful visual insights, allowing users to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), evaluate product performance, identify high-value customers, and support data-driven business decisions.

## 🎯 Business Problem

Businesses need a centralized dashboard to monitor sales performance, customer activity, and product performance in real time. Without effective visualization, decision-makers may struggle to identify revenue trends, high-performing products, and valuable customers.

This Power BI dashboard answers key business questions such as:

- What is the total sales revenue?
- Which products generate the highest revenue?
- Who are the top customers?
- How are sales performing over time?
- Which categories contribute the most to revenue?
- What KPIs best summarize business performance?

- ## 📂 Dataset

This dashboard uses the Northwind sample database, which contains transactional sales data across multiple related tables.

The analysis combines data from:

- Orders
- Order Details
- Customers
- Products
- Employees
- Shippers

The relationships between these tables enable a complete analysis of sales performance and customer activity.

## 📈 Key Insights

### Sales Performance

- The dashboard provides a high-level overview of Total Sales, Net Sales, Total Orders, and Total Quantity Sold.
- KPI cards allow users to quickly evaluate overall business performance.

### Employee Performance

- Sales performance can be analyzed by employee to identify top contributors.
- Employee filters enable comparison between different sales representatives.

### Customer Analysis

- The dashboard highlights customer purchasing activity.
- Users can identify high-value customers by filtering sales performance.

### Geographic Analysis

- Sales are analyzed by shipping country to compare regional performance.
- Country filters allow users to explore sales across different markets.

### Time Trend Analysis

- The sales trend chart tracks Net Sales across different years and quarters.
- This helps identify seasonal patterns and business growth over time.
